# DiveCast Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

## What data we collect

DiveCast collects the following data to provide dive forecasting and logging:

- **Account information**: Email address and password (stored securely by Supabase)
- **Location data**: GPS coordinates of dive spots you save or log
- **Dive logs**: Ratings, fish catches, conditions, notes, and photos you enter
- **Trophy records**: Species, weight, and photos of trophy fish

## How we use your data

- To provide personalized dive spot recommendations
- To display weather, tide, and solunar forecasts for your saved spots
- To store your dive history and trophy collection
- To improve forecast accuracy based on your logged conditions

## Third-party services

- **Supabase**: Database and authentication (EU hosted)
- **Met.no**: Weather and tide data from the Norwegian Meteorological Institute
- **Open-Meteo**: Elevation data for wind shelter analysis
- **Anthropic Claude API**: AI-powered dive insights (when you choose to use it)
- **Copernicus Marine Service**: Satellite algae data (optional)

## Data storage

Your data is stored in Supabase (cloud database) and protected by Row Level Security — only you can access your own data. Photos are stored in Supabase Storage.

Local preferences (filters, favorites) are stored on your device using AsyncStorage.

## Your rights

You can:
- View all your data within the app
- Delete individual dives, spots, and trophies
- Delete your account by contacting us
- Export your data by contacting us

## Data sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties. Third-party services only receive the minimum data needed (coordinates for weather lookups, dive logs for AI analysis when you choose to use it).
