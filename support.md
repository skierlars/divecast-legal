## Common questions

### How do I log a dive?

Open the **Explore** tab and long-press anywhere on the map to drop a pin. Choose "Log dive", fill in the details, and save. Weather and tide conditions at the time of the dive are captured automatically.

### Why are no spots showing on the Today tab?

DiveCast ranks spots you've previously logged or saved. The first time you open the app, it will be empty — head to Explore and add your first spot.

### Tides look wrong for my location

DiveCast currently uses tide data from three Norwegian harbours: Sandnes, Stavanger, and Sirevåg. Tide values are interpolated from the nearest harbour. If you're outside the southwest coast of Norway, tide data will be less accurate.

### The AI features are not responding

AI Dive Insights and AI Week Planner require a network connection and a logged-in account. If the feature has not worked for several hours, email us so we can check the backend.

### How do I delete my account?

Go to **Settings** → **Delete account** and follow the instructions, or email tannlegenordbo@gmail.com and we'll remove your account and all associated data within 7 days.

### Where is my data stored?

Your dive logs, photos, and spots are stored in Supabase (EU-hosted). Details are in our [Privacy Policy](./).
